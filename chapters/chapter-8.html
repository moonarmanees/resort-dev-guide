Chapter 8: Construction and Development Works

The construction phase involves the physical realization of the resort. It requires careful planning, logistics, and management, especially given the remote island locations.

Key Construction Stages

  • Mobilization: The contractor transports equipment, materials, and labor to the island. Temporary facilities (labor camps, storage) are set up.
  • Site Preparation: Clearing vegetation (as per EIA), setting out building locations.
  • Foundation and Structural Works: Construction of foundations, columns, beams, and slabs for all buildings.
  • MEP First Fix: Installation of concealed plumbing, electrical conduits, and ductwork.
  • Finishing Works: Plastering, tiling, painting, and installation of doors, windows, and fixtures.
  • Exterior and Landscaping: Construction of pools, decks, pathways, and planting of vegetation.
