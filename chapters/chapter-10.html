Chapter 10: Pre-Opening and Systems Commissioning

The pre-opening phase is a critical transition period where the completed physical asset is transformed into a functioning, guest-ready resort. This phase typically begins 6-9 months before the scheduled opening date.

Key Pre-Opening Activities

  • Staff Recruitment and Training: Hiring the resort's operational team, from the General Manager to front-line staff.
  • Procurement of FF&E and OS&E: Purchasing Furniture, Fixtures & Equipment and Operating Supplies & Equipment.
  • Marketing and Sales Launch: Initiating marketing campaigns and opening bookings.
  • Soft Opening: A trial period with a limited number of guests to test systems and service flows.

Systems Commissioning

Commissioning is the process of testing and verifying that all building systems function as designed. This includes:

  • Power generation and distribution systems.
  • Water desalination and distribution.
  • Wastewater treatment plant.
  • HVAC systems.
  • Fire alarm and safety systems.
  • IT and communication networks.
