Chapter 11: Official Opening and Operational Transition

The official opening marks the culmination of the development effort. A smooth handover from the project team to the operations team is essential for long-term success.

Official Opening

  • Final Inspections: Obtaining the final occupancy permits and the Resort Operating License from the Ministry of Tourism.
  • Grand Opening Event: A marketing event to generate publicity and officially launch the resort.
  • Ramp-Up Period: The initial months of operation where occupancy gradually increases to stabilized levels.

Operational Handover

The handover process involves the formal transfer of responsibility and information. Key documents to be handed over include:

  • As-Built Drawings: Updated construction drawings showing the final, completed state of the resort.
  • Operations and Maintenance (O&M) Manuals: For all installed equipment and systems.
  • Warranties and Guarantees: From the contractor and equipment suppliers.
  • Final Accounts: A complete record of all project costs.
