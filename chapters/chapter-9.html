Chapter 9: On-Site Construction Management and Oversight

Effective on-site management is crucial for monitoring progress, ensuring quality, and resolving issues promptly. This is typically led by the Project Manager and a site-based team.

Key Management Activities

  • Progress Monitoring: Regular site inspections and meetings to track progress against the project schedule.
  • Quality Assurance/Quality Control (QA/QC): Inspecting workmanship and materials to ensure they meet the project specifications and quality standards.
  • Health, Safety, and Environment (HSE) Management: Enforcing safety protocols to prevent accidents and ensuring compliance with the EIA.
  • Change Order Management: Managing any changes to the project scope, schedule, or cost in a structured manner.
  • Reporting: Providing regular progress reports to the owner and other stakeholders.
