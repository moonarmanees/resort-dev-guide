Foreword [cite: 3]

[cite_start]

This guide serves as a practical roadmap for anyone planning, managing, or overseeing the development of a resort in the Maldives—whether you are an owner, investor, consultant, architect, engineer, or contractor. [cite: 31]

[cite_start]

Drawing on insights from seasoned professionals in both the private sector and public institutions—including policymakers, legal advisors, environmental experts, and financial planners—this guide presents a step-by-step overview of the resort development process. [cite: 32]

[cite_start]

It spans from land acquisition through regulatory approvals, design and construction, all the way to the official resort opening and operational handover. [cite: 33]
